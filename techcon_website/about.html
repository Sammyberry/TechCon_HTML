Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of developers and tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global tech conferences, hosting thousands of participants annually.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, promote collaboration, and drive technological growth across industries. We are committed to creating a diverse, inclusive space for tech professionals to share knowledge and spark change.

Notable Past Speakers

As the founder of CodeVerse Africa, Jamal shared insights into tech education initiatives and startup acceleration at TechCon 2021.

A cybersecurity expert and TED speaker, Sofia presented a deep dive into privacy resilience in an age of data breaches at TechCon 2020.
